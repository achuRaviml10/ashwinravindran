About Company

AR Studios is a renowned animation and film production company based in Kerala, India, specializing in the Malayalam film industry. Established with a vision to bring folk tales and legendary narratives to life, AR Studios has garnered a loyal following and attracted top talents from various entertainment sectors. The studio is known for its meticulous adaptation of classic books and novels, creating captivating stories that resonate with diverse audiences worldwide

AR Studios operates as a comprehensive studio and production house, engaging in a broad spectrum of projects including animated films, television series, and innovative storytelling ventures. With a global outlook, the company collaborates on projects across continents, enriching the world of entertainment with unique narratives and visual experiences.
